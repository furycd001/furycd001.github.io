Awesome Arch

February 8, 2022

Arch Linux is a Linux distribution thats lightweight & tries to Keep things Simple. It can be installed as a minimal base system & then configured from the ground up by the user. The user can create their own environment, through installing simply only what they may require. Awesome Arch is a list of awesome Arch related projects.
