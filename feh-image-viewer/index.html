Feh image viewer.

May 5, 2023

Feh is a lightweight and fast image viewer for Linux and Unix-like systems. It’s a versatile tool that can display a wide range of image formats, including JPEG, PNG, TIFF, and GIF, among many others. Feh has a simple and intuitive interface that makes it easy to use, and it’s highly customizable through command-line options and configuration files.

One of the most notable features of feh is its ability to display images in a slideshow mode. This mode can be customized to display images in a particular order, at a specific interval, and with various effects and transitions. Feh also supports a range of image manipulation options, such as zooming, rotating, and flipping images.

Another advantage of feh is its small memory footprint. Feh is designed to be lightweight and fast, making it an excellent choice for users with low-end systems or those who prefer a minimalist approach.

Overall, feh is a great image viewer for users who value speed, simplicity, and flexibility. Its extensive feature set, combined with its lightweight design, makes it an excellent choice for anyone looking for a powerful image viewer for their Linux or Unix-like system.

One thing to note: Whenever you are browsing through a bunch of images, you can press CTRL+delete to delete the images from disk.
